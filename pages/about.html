Our Timeline


1. We started on 24th January 2017 when we purchased the land.
2. In the middle of 2017, we made the deal for the machine from Düsseldorf, Germany.
3. The first container arrived in mid-2018.
4. We began rebuilding the machine in 2020.
5. Finally, on 22nd January 2024, we started production.

Our Vision

To establish a leading name in Egypt and the Middle East in the paper recycling industry, driving sustainable solutions and pioneering environmental stewardship in the region

Our Mission


We strive to recycle paper efficiently and responsibly, reducing waste and protecting our environment.
